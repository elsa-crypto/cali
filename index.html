The hardest part of meditating is starting: opening another app, browsing a library, picking between forty sessions, staring at a screen one swipe away from your notifications. A phone call skips all of it — you close your eyes and follow a voice. That's the whole thing.

How it works
1

Text when you're ready

No forms, no menus. Just text like you would a friend — before bed, after work, between meetings, whenever you want a reset.

2

Say yes to a call

Cali checks what kind of session you're in the mood for, then asks before dialing. It never calls out of nowhere.

3

Close your eyes and breathe

A quick hello, then the meditation begins — breathing, unwinding, focus, or sleep, guided live and made for how you feel today, as long as you want it.

4

Land softly

A gentle check-in on how you feel, and you're on with your day. Cali remembers what worked for next time.
