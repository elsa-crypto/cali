"The Snipe" — the Cali call · golden hour → sunset

Battery Spencer overlook — Golden Gate Bridge below, city beyond

  • The cut: from black (bus CU) smash to locked-off wide — bridge, amber light, wind only. Record 30s of clean atmosphere first; the silence is the edit's payload.

02 Locations & logistics

Finale location

SpotBattery Spencer, Marin Headlands (GGNRA)
AddressConzelman Rd, Sausalito, CA 94965 · Plus code RGM9+4V
WhyHighest close-up angle on the bridge — towers at eye level, city skyline behind. Sunset light hits the talent and the bridge face.
WindCold and real, even July. Jacket that reads on camera; hair plan; wind muff on every mic.
Fog planJuly evenings Karl the Fog often swallows the towers. If fog: shoot it. Ghost-bridge at 1/30 is on-theme (calm ≠ postcard). Full whiteout backup: Fort Point / Crissy Field on the SF side, usually below the fog deck.
PermitsHandheld/tripod, crew of 2–3, public overlook — low-impact, no permit needed. Don't block the path or fly a drone (GGNRA: prohibited).

Camera recipe (whole film)

Base24 fps · Manual exposure · ISO 100 · f/4–f/8 outdoors
Chaos1/15 handheld (1/8 for one extreme take per scene, 1/30 if too mushy)
Calm1/48 on tripod — the world stops smearing when Cali answers
NDVariable ND (ND16–ND64) mandatory in daylight at 1/15; off by ~7:45 PM
Grade refChaos = fluorescent green cast · Calm = amber/rose → blue hour
FramingChaos: tilted, tight, faces half-blocked · Calm: level, negative space, huge sky

Gear checklist

  • Camera + 2 lenses (24–35mm chaos · 70mm+ for finale profile)
  • Variable ND (the day does not work without it)
  • Tripod (stays in car until the move)
  • 4+ batteries, 2+ cards, card reader for wrap backup
  • Audio recorder + lav (wind muff!) — wild ambience at every location
  • Talent kit: layers/jacket, hair ties, water, tote prop, phone charger brick
  • Print this sheet — Headlands cell signal is spotty

Sound design capture list

  • Cable car bell + Powell St walla (Sc. 1)
  • Stockton St market shouts, register beeps (Sc. 2)
  • BART escalator drone, crosswalk chirp (Sc. 3)
  • Bus interior rattle + tunnel screech (Sc. 4)
  • 30s clean wind + distant surf at Battery Spencer (Sc. 5 — the silence that makes the snipe land)
  • Record Cali V.O. line separately in a quiet room; phone-EQ it in post
